# Weapons and Monsters Reference (DCSS-based)

This document lists weapon types and monster categories from Dungeon Crawl Stone Soup for reference when designing Wasteland mod content.

---

## WEAPON TYPES

### Short Blades
**Characteristics:** Fast, low damage, good with stabbing
- **Dagger** - Fastest, lowest damage (base: 4)
- **Quick Blade** - Very fast (base: 5)
- **Short Sword** - Balanced starter (base: 5)
- **Rapier** - Precise strikes (base: 7)

### Long Blades
**Characteristics:** Balanced speed/damage, versatile
- **Falchion** - Entry long blade (base: 7)
- **Long Sword** - Classic warrior weapon (base: 8)
- **Scimitar** - Fast curved blade (base: 9)
- **Great Sword** - Two-handed power (base: 13)
- **Demon Blade** - Rare, powerful (base: 13)
- **Katana** - Rare, precise (base: 10)
- **Double Sword** - Two-handed speed (base: 15)
- **Triple Sword** - Legendary (base: 17)

### Axes
**Characteristics:** High damage, cleave multiple enemies
- **Hand Axe** - Throwable, basic (base: 7)
- **War Axe** - Standard melee axe (base: 11)
- **Broad Axe** - Heavy two-hander (base: 13)
- **Battleaxe** - Classic heavy weapon (base: 15)
- **Executioner's Axe** - Massive damage (base: 18)

### Maces & Flails
**Characteristics:** Good vs armored foes, crushing damage
- **Club** - Primitive (base: 5)
- **Whip** - Ranged reach (base: 6)
- **Mace** - Basic bludgeon (base: 8)
- **Flail** - Chained weapon (base: 10)
- **Morningstar** - Spiked ball (base: 13)
- **Demon Whip** - Rare, powerful (base: 11)
- **Great Mace** - Two-handed crusher (base: 17)
- **Giant Club** - Ogre weapon (base: 20)
- **Giant Spiked Club** - Brutal (base: 22)

### Polearms
**Characteristics:** Reach attacks, good positioning
- **Spear** - Basic reach weapon (base: 6)
- **Trident** - Three-pronged (base: 9)
- **Halberd** - Axe-spear hybrid (base: 13)
- **Scythe** - Farming tool weapon (base: 14)
- **Glaive** - Bladed pole (base: 15)
- **Bardiche** - Heavy poleaxe (base: 18)
- **Demon Trident** - Rare, fiery (base: 12)

### Staves
**Characteristics:** Quarterstaff fighting, magical variants
- **Staff** - Basic quarterstaff (base: 5)
- **Lajatang** - Double-ended staff (base: 16)
- **Magical Staves** - Elemental damage variants (fire, ice, earth, air, poison, death)

### Ranged Weapons

#### Bows
- **Shortbow** - Fast, low damage (base: 9)
- **Longbow** - Powerful, slower (base: 15)

#### Crossbows
- **Hand Crossbow** - One-handed (base: 12)
- **Arbalest** - Heavy crossbow (base: 18)
- **Triple Crossbow** - Rare, multi-shot (base: 22)

#### Slings
- **Hunting Sling** - Basic ranged (base: 5)
- **Fustibalus** - Heavy sling (base: 8)

#### Throwing
- **Throwing Net** - Entangle enemies
- **Boomerang** - Returns to thrower
- **Javelin** - Heavy throwing spear
- **Large Rock** - Giant ammunition

### Unarmed Combat
**Characteristics:** Scaling with skill and mutations
- **Bare Hands** - Base unarmed
- **Claws** - From mutations (1-3 levels)
- **Tentacles** - Mutation variant
- **Talons** - Bird-like claws

## WASTELAND ADAPTATIONS (✅ IMPLEMENTED!)

### Wasteland Weapons (✅ IN GAME)
All wasteland-themed weapons are now implemented in WeaponType.java:

**Makeshift/Melee:**
- Shiv, Combat Knife, Machete (short blades)
- Lead Pipe, Crowbar, Baseball Bat, Sledgehammer, Pneumatic Hammer (maces/flails)
- Rusty Sword, Scrap Blade, Katana, Ripper (long blades)
- Hatchet, Fire Axe, Super Sledge (axes)
- Spear, Pitchfork, Halberd (polearms)

**Guns (REPLACES SLINGS):**
- **Tier 1 Pistols:** Pipe Pistol, 22 Pistol
- **Tier 2 Pistols:** 9mm Pistol, 45 Pistol
- **Tier 3 Pistols:** 44 Magnum, Desert Eagle
- **Tier 4 Rifles:** Hunting Rifle, Varmint Rifle
- **Tier 5 Combat Rifles:** Assault Rifle, Battle Rifle, Sniper Rifle
- **Tier 6 Shotguns:** Sawed-Off Shotgun, Combat Shotgun
- **Tier 7 Energy Weapons:** Laser Pistol, Laser Rifle, Plasma Rifle, Gauss Rifle

**Ammunition (REPLACES STONES):**
- 22LR, 9mm, 45 ACP, 5.56mm, 7.62mm, Shotgun Shells, Energy Cells
- Grenades, Molotovs, Pipe Bombs

**Skills Updated:**
- SLINGS skill replaced with GUNS skill (Skill.java:22)
- Kobold race has +2 aptitude for GUNS (formerly SLINGS)
